[Pre-Verse]
A song to the ascents,
The toiling of the endless content[s].

[Verse 1]
I raise my eyes to the mountains;
A parable, incomparable, the bulwarks of challenge;
By what strength do I endure?
From whence will come my help?

[Verse 2]
My help is from Hashem,
Maker of heaven and earth;
Bringer of rain, molder of mountains,
The omnipotent creator of challenges!

[Humming]

[Verse 3]
As you tread these narrow paths,
He will not allow the faltering of your foot;
Ready to catch you should you stumble -
Not slumber will your Guardian.

[Verse 4]
It is so! Neither slumbers nor sleeps -
A vigil - a vigil he keeps,
Never gave up the ghost,
Nor abandoned his post -
The Guardian, O' the Guardian of Israel.

[Verse 5]
Hashem is your Guardian;
Steadfast toil is key and,
When your best is not enough, 
And the challenge seems too tough,

Sending others providing aid to help you withstand,
Hashem is your protective shade at your right hand.

[Verse 6]
Fear not what the other nations say,
Nor shadowed schemes of heretics, for by day 
The sun will not harm you, Nor the moon by night.

[Humming]

[Verse 7]
From insidious speech, to untithed grain,
Hashem will protect you from every evil;
When returned at night, and He returns it each morning,
He will guard your soul.

[Verse 8]
When ceasing on your endless work,
The curse of old prevailing,
Hashem will guard your departure and your arrival,
From this time until eternity!
